About

"Strategy without numbers is a guess. Numbers without strategy are just accounting."

The unusual combination is the point.

I spent my first decade as a chief accountant and tax specialist — building the kind of commercial instinct that can't be taught in a workshop. Then I crossed into digital. Not because the numbers bored me, but because I saw how few digital leaders actually understood them.

Today I work as a Digital Director — advising on strategy, digital transformation, and commercial positioning. I bring something most consultants don't: I can sit in the boardroom and talk P&L, then walk into the creative studio and talk channel architecture. Both rooms trust me.

That's the Acutis proposition. Not a generalist who dabbles. A specialist who bridged two worlds — and knows exactly where the gaps are.
